Walter's Bakery

Walter's Bakery has been around since the mid 90's. We are proud to server the Concord, NC and greater area. From its inception, Walter's Bakery is known as bakery unique for our square cupcakes and cakes. We take pride in using natural ingredients in our cupcakes, cakes, desserts and more.

Our vision is to create upscale, quick-serve bakery with a focus on simple and satisfying souther style desserts and baked goods. We also deliver to your location.
